Trump reposts Modi's support for Gaza peace plan without adding his own words

  • Donald Trump reshared PM Modi's endorsement of his Gaza peace plan, which Modi called a "viable pathway" to lasting peace and security, urging all parties to unite behind Trump's initiative.
  • US President Donald Trump reshared Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s message backing his new plan to end the Gaza conflict. However, Trump did not add any words of his own, simply reposting Modi’s endorsement on Truth Social.
  • In his post, Modi said India “welcomes President Donald J. Trump’s announcement of a comprehensive plan to end the Gaza conflict.”
